Regalia First Credit Card

First Level towards Indulgence


All You Need To Know...

1. Travel Benefits

    Airport Lounge Access within India

  • 8 complimentary access per calendar year within India ( both domestic and international terminal) using Visa / MasterCard Regalia First Credit Card
  • Visits exceeding the complimentary quota will be allowed at the discretion of lounge and will also be chargeable by the lounge
  • Visa Customers will be charged a transaction fee of Rs. 2 on their Visa Regalia First Credit card towards complimentary lounge access

    • Airport Lounge Access outside India

  • Customers can apply for Priority Pass for self or /and add on members post doing 4 retail transaction on their PIONEER Bank Regalia First Credit.
  • Using Priority Pass, you and your add on member can together avail up to 3 complimentary access per calendar year outside India.
  • Customers exceeding the 3 complimentary visit would be charged at US $27 + GST per visit
  • These charges will be billed on your subsequent statement within 90 days of date of visit & this transaction will not accrue Reward Points .
  • The currency conversion rate would be as per the exchange rate on the day of billing.

2. Dining Experience

  • Experience the Good Food Trail dining program at premium restaurants across top cities.

3. Contactless Payment

  • The PIONEER Bank Regalia First Credit Card is enabled for contactless payments, facilitating fast, convenient and secure payments at retail outlets.
  • To see if your Card is contactless, look for the contactless network symbol on your Card.

4. Eligibility

    For Salaried Indian national

  • Minimum age 21 yrs and Maximum age 60 Years.
  • Gross Monthly Income of more than Rs 40,000

    • For Self Employed Indian national

  • Minimum age 21 yrs and Maximum age 65 Years.
  • ITR of more than Rs 5 lakh per annum

5. Rewards Program

    Rewards Accrual

  • You earn 4 reward points on Rs. 150, on all retail* spends including Insurance, Utilities, Education and RentPay.

    • Annual Spends Benefit

  • Earn 5,000 Reward Points on annual spends of Rs. 3,00,000 or more in each anniversary year*
  • Earn additional 2,500 Reward Points on annual spends of Rs. 6,00,000 or more in each anniversary year*

    • Exclusive Travel Portal

  • Airline ticket booking.
  • Hotel booking

6. Enjoy Accelerated Reward Points

  • Flights and hotel bookings via Smartbuy at a value of 1 RP = Rs 0.30
  • Airmiles conversion through netbanking at a value of 1RP = 0.3 airmile
  • Products and Vouchers via Netbanking or SmartBuy at a value of 1 RP = upto Rs 0.25
  • Cashback at a value of 1 RP = Rs 0.15​​​​​​​

7. Comprehensive Protection

  • Accidental air death cover worth Rs. 50 lakhs
  • Emergency overseas hospitalization up to Rs. 10 lakhs in case of emergency
  • Lost card liability cover of upto Rs 5 lakhs​​​​​​​

8. Fees and Charges

  • First Year Membership Fee - Rs. 1000/- + Applicable Taxes, Renewal Membership Fee – Rs. 1000/- + Applicable Taxes
  • Welcome Benefit of 1000 Reward Points and renewal benefit of 1000 Reward Points (applicable only after the membership fee is realized and not applicable when the fee is waived off)* Terms & Conditions Apply
  • Spend Rs. 1,00,000 in 12 Months and get Renewal Fee waived for next renewal year.
