Regalia Credit Card

Luxurious Indulgences. Ready For You


All You Need To Know...

1. Key Features

  • 12 Complimentary Airport Lounge Access within India and 6 outside India.
  • Bonus 10,000 Reward Points on Rs. 5 lakhs spends and additional 5,000 Reward Points on Rs. 8 lakhs spends in an anniversary year.
  • Earn 4 Reward Points on every Rs.150 spent (On all retail* spends)

2. Additional Features

  • Zero Lost card liability : In the unfortunate event of losing your PIONEER Bank Regalia Credit Card, on reporting it immediately to our 24-hour call centre, you have zero liability on any fraudulent transactions made on your Credit Card.
  • Foreign Currency Markup : 2% on all your foreign currency spends.
  • Revolving Credit : Enjoy Revolving Credit on your PIONEER Bank Regalia Credit Card at nominal interest rate.

3. Contactless Payment

  • The PIONEER Bank Regalia Credit Card is enabled for contactless payments, facilitating fast, convenient and secure payments at retail outlets.
  • To see if your Card is contactless, look for the contactless network symbol on your Card.

4. Eligibility

    For Salaried Indian national

  • Age: Min 21 years & Max 60 Years
  • Net Monthly Income > Rs 1 Lakh

    • For Self Employed Indian national

  • Age: Min 21 years & Max 65 Years
  • ITR > Rs 12 Lakhs per annum

5. Dining Benefit

  • Assured flat 25% off at 2000+ premium restaurants
  • 1+1 on buffet at 200+ restaurants
  • Additional 5% off and Zero convenience fee.
  • Early access to events and festivals such as Gourmetlicious

6. Milestone Benefit

  • Earn Bonus 10,000 Reward Points when you spend Rs. 5 lakhs in the anniversary year.
  • Earn additional 5,000 Reward Points when you spend Rs. 8 lakhs spends in the anniversary year

7. Renewal Offer

  • Renewal fee waived off if you spend more than 3L in the previous year​​​​​​​

8. Smart EMI

  • PIONEER Bank Regalia Credit Card comes with an option to convert your big spends into EMI after purchase.

9. Fees & Charges

  • Joining/ Renewal Membership Fee - Rs. 2500/- + Applicable Taxes.
  • Welcome Benefit of 2500 Reward Points and renewal benefit of 2500 Reward Points (applicable only after the membership fee is realized and not applicable when the fee is waived off)
  • The Bank reserves the right to cancel the Card in case the Card remains inactive and is not used for effecting any transaction for a continuous period of 6 (Six) months after prior written notice sent on the email address and/or phone number and/or communication address, registered in the records of the Bank.
