Jet Privilege Platinum Card

Fly FREE faster with the best in class airline co-brand credit card.


All You Need To Know...

1. Key Features

  • Get 4 JPMiles per Rs.150 that you spend (only on eligible retail transactions).
  • Earn JPMiles on transactions made on JetPrivilege.

2. Additional Features

  • Start your JPMiles journey with up to 3,000 JPMiles.
  • Get 1,500 JPMiles when you swipe your card for the first time (on transactions of Rs.150 or more) within 90 days of your card being set up.
  • Spend Rs.50,000 or more within 90 days of your card being set up and earn another 1,500 JPMiles.

3. Contactless Payments

  • Enjoy contactless payment facility with the card so you can just tap and go.

4. Eligibility

    For Salaried Indian national

  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Maximum age: 60 years

    • For Self Employed Indian national

  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Maximum age: 65 years

5. Lounge Access

  • Access to domestic lounges at select airports in India.
  • Visa Lounge Programme: Up to 2 free visits each year at participating domestic airport lounges.

6. Insurance Coverage

  • Up to Rs.50 lakh complimentary accidental death insurance coverage.
  • Complimentary fire and burglary protection for a period of 180 days from the date of purchase.
  • Rs.25,000 for personal portable equipment.
  • Up to Rs.2 lakh for all other assets.

7. Renewal Benefits

  • Earn up to 1,500 JPMiles when you use your card within 90 days of your membership being renewed (minimum transaction amount is Rs.150).

8. Interest-Free Credit and Revolving Credit Facility

  • You get up to 50 days of interest-free credit with this card and a revolving credit facility with an interest rate of 3.49% per month.

9. Fees & Charges

  • Joining fee - Rs.1,000 + applicable taxes
  • Annual fee - Rs.1,000 + applicable taxes (second year onwards, get a waiver on the annual fee if you spend Rs.2,50,000 in the previous year)
  • Minimum repayment - 5% or Rs.200, whichever is higher
  • Cash advance limit - 40% of the credit limit
  • Revolving credit interest - 3.49% per month
  • Reissue of lost or stolen card - Rs.100
  • Foreign currency mark-up fee - 3.5% of the transacted amount
