Platinum Times Card

Best Entertainment Co-brand card


All You Need To Know...

1. Key Features

  • Get a bouquet of discount vouchers as a welcome gift.
  • Enjoy a discount of 25% on movies.
  • Make the most of dining at more than 1,000 partner restaurants with a discount of 20%.

2. Additional Features

  • Get accelerated reward points on dining during weekdays.
  • The renewal fee of the card is waived if the annual expenditure is more than Rs.2,50,000.

3. Welcome Benefit

  • Kickstart your journey of entertainment and food with a range of vouchers on apparel, shopping, dining and other various categories offered by the bank.

4. Eligibility

    For Salaried Indian national

  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Maximum age: 60 years

    • For Self Employed Indian national

  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Maximum age: 65 years

5. Movie and Dining Benefits

  • Get a wide range of city-based offers and discounts on dining, movie tickets, online shopping, etc.
  • Apart from this, you will get up to 25% off on movie ticket bookings.
  • Enjoy a discount of 20% on dining all throughout the year with your credit card at partner restaurants.

6. Insurance Benefits

  • For a minimum of 4 transactions in a month, avail a free Accidental Death insurance worth Rs.50 lakh.
  • Get a free Fire and Burglary protection for goods above Rs.5,000 for 6 months.
  • Once you report the loss, cardholders carry zero liability on any fraudulent transactions.

7. Reward Point Benefits

  • With the PIONEER Bank Platinum Times credit card, get 3 reward points for every Rs.150 spent.
  • Avail accelerated rewards of 10 reward points for every Rs.150 spent on dining during weekdays.
  • The reward points will be valid for 2 years from the date of accumulation.

8. Fuel Benefits

  • With the credit card, you will also be getting a fuel surcharge waiver of 1.5% for fuel transactions.
  • The maximum waiver per statement cycle is Rs.250.

9. Fees & Charges

  • Annual fee -Rs.1,000
  • Minimum repayment - 5% or a minimum of Rs.200
  • Cash advance limit - 40% of the credit limit
  • Interest-free period - Upto 50 days.
  • Revolving credit interest - 3.49% per month
  • Additional card fee - Free
  • Cash advance charges - 2.5% on the withdrawn amount or Rs.500; the higher amount is taken.
  • Railway ticket purchase fee - 1.8% of the transaction amount + GST
