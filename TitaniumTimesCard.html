Titanium Times Card

Great Times. Ready For You


All You Need To Know...

1. Key Features

  • Entertainment benefits : 25% off on Movie Tickets on BookMyShow. Save up to Rs 350 per transaction!.
  • Shopping & Lifestyle benefits : Amazing round-the-year discount offers on dining, wellness, shopping and travel at selected partners.

2. Additional Features

  • Utility Bill Payments : Register your Credit Card with SmartPay, HDFC Bank's Utility Bill payment service. You can then ensure that all your utility bills are paid on time, conveniently and easily.
  • Zero Lost Card Liability : In the infortunate event if you lose your Credit Card, report it immediately to our 24-hour call centre. After reporting the loss, you carry zero liability on any fraudulent transactions made with your card.
  • Interest Free Credit Period : Up to 50 days^ of interest free credit period on your Credit Card from the date of purchase (^subject to the submission of the charge by the Merchant)
  • Revolving Credit : Available at a nominal interest rate on your Credit Card.

3. Welcome Benefit

  • Gift Vouchers across shopping, apparel, dining and many more categories.

4. Eligibility

    For Salaried Indian national

  • Age: 21 years to 60 years
  • Net monthly income: Rs 25,000 & above

    • For Self Employed Indian national

  • Age: 21 years to 65 years
  • Annual Income should be Rs 6,00,000 & above

5. Contactless Payment

  • The PIONEER Bank Titanium Times Card is enabled for contactless payments, facilitating fast, convenient and secure payments at retail outlets.
  • To see if your credit card is contactless, look for the contactless network symbol on your card

6. Reward Point/CashBack Redemption & Validity

  • Earn 2 Reward Points for every ₹150 spent.
  • Weekday Dining Bonanza Reward Points
  • Earn 5 Reward Points* on every ₹150 spent on dining spends on weekdays

7. Renewal Offer

  • Spend ₹ 1,50,000 in a year and get a waiver of renewal membership fee.

8. Smart EMI

  • Your Credit Card comes with an option to convert your big spends into EMI after purchase.

9. Fees & Charges

  • Membership Fee- ₹ 500 (First Year) along with applicable taxes
  • Renewal Membership Fee- ₹ 500 along with applicable taxes
  • Revolving credit limit- As per Bank’s Norms
  • Interest-free period - Upto 50 days.
  • Revolving credit interest - 3.49% per month
  • Additional card fee - Free
  • Cash advance charges - 2.5% on the withdrawn amount or Rs.500; the higher amount is taken.
